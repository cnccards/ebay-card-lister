About
How it works:
• You upload photos of a card
• Google Gemini AI identifies it
• The app generates a fully-filled eBay listing
• You review/edit, then add to your list
• When ready, export all cards as a CSV to upload to eBay

Cost: Free (Gemini free tier gives 250 requests/day)
